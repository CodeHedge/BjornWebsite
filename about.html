About Us

Learn about this website and our mission to support the Bjorn Cyber Viking community

Our Story

This website was created by a passionate fan of the Bjorn Cyber Viking project who wanted to help the community flourish by compiling comprehensive resources in one convenient location. We believe that knowledge is power, and in the digital age, understanding cybersecurity is essential for protecting ourselves and our communities.

Our Mission

Education

We're committed to making cybersecurity education accessible to everyone, from beginners to advanced practitioners, by providing comprehensive resources about Bjorn.

Resource Compilation

Creating a centralized hub where all Bjorn-related resources, tutorials, 3D models, and community links can be easily found. Have suggestions? Contact us!, email me and i'll add it to the site!

Our Values

Innovation

We support the innovative spirit of the Bjorn project and the community's constant push to advance cybersecurity tools.

Education

We believe in learning by doing and providing comprehensive resources for skill development in cybersecurity.

Collaboration

We foster an environment where community members can share knowledge and support each other in their cybersecurity journey.

Ethics

We promote responsible and ethical use of security tools for educational and authorized testing purposes only.

Get in Touch
